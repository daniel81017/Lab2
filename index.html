Walthamstow, London

Walthamstow is a borough in the northeast of Greater London. There are various attractions in Walthamstow, namely the market that takes place most days on the pedestrianized High Street. This area is more lively on Sundays as the Sunday Market offers "fresh street food, community entertainment and local brands." To find out more about the Sunday Market and Social, click here.

To be a vendor in either of these events, one must register for a street trading licence with the London Borough of Waltham Forest (the area's local council).
