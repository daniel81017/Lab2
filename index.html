Walthamstow, London


Walthamstow is a borough in the northeast of Greater London. Though largely a commuter and bedroom suburb, the Walthamstow Market takes place most days on the High Street. This area is more lively on Sundays as the Sunday Market offers "fresh street food, community entertainment and local brands." To find out more about the Sunday Market and Social, click here.

This map contains an outline of the neighbourhood immediately surrounding Walthamstow High Street. Furthermore, there is an outline of the street itself. The bright red, meanwhile, outlines the buildings that front onto High Street.

The three black circles immediately south of Walthamstow High Street are the nearest rail stations to the area, with Underground and Overground services operated by Transport for London (TfL). Click on the circular icons to see station name information!
